FoodHood

Reducing foodwase by redistributing leftover foods on your daily routes

The waste of edible food is a problem of global importance. Especially in western industrial countries, a huge amount of the food waste is produced on consumer level. FoodHood aimed at tackling this problem by providing consumers with a tool that allows them to donate food before it spoils, and organize its transportation. FoodHood was awared as one of the best projects of the term of its creation.
